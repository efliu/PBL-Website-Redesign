Internal Networking

While not a committee that takes applicants, the Internal Networking committee is responsible for fostering the social environment of the organization. From planning the semesterly Member's Retreat to organizing countless social events throughout the semester, the Internal Networking committee works hard to encourage a tight-knight PBL community.

Events

From ice skating and dodgeball to weekend outings to Santa Cruz or Lake Tahoe, our Internal Networking chairs prepare social events of all kinds throughout the semester.



Upper Division

The Upper Division committee spearheads PBL's consulting services. Working with a wide array of clients from budding start-ups to Fortune 500 firms, Upper Division teams allow members to take the knowledge and experience from their industry-specific committee curriculums and apply them to real world projects.

Executive

The Executive team manages the smooth day-to-day operations of the organization while planning the semester's large-scale events and activities. From co-hosting speaker panels to leading our premier professional development workshops, the Executive team embodies the PBL values and the hallmarks of personal and professional success.
